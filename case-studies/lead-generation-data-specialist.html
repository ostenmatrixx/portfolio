Case Study

Lead Generation Data Ops

Role: Lead Generation & Data Specialist (Contract) Timeline: Mar 2023 - Jan 2024

Problem

Lead lists from multiple sources needed higher quality and better validation.

Approach

Defined screening criteria, verified contact records, and maintained structured spreadsheets with quality checks.

Result

Cleaner lead data, improved reliability for outreach teams, and fewer errors downstream.

Tools Used

Online research tools, spreadsheet workflows, QA validation steps.
